Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top-right corner of the UlyBilim homepage. Fill in your details including name, email, and create a password. After submitting, check your email for a verification link to activate your account.

Once you're logged in, browse the course catalog and select the course you're interested in. On the course page, click the "Enroll Now" button. If it's a paid course, you'll be directed to the payment gateway. For free courses, you'll get immediate access.

Yes, UlyBilim offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not satisfied with the course content, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchase through your account dashboard under "My Purchases" > "Request Refund."

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete lessons. You can view your overall progress in the course dashboard. Each course has a progress bar showing the percentage of completion, and you can see which modules you've completed and which ones are still pending.

Advanced courses may have prerequisites, which will be clearly mentioned in the course description. The system will also notify you if you're missing any prerequisites when attempting to enroll. We recommend checking the "Course Requirements" section before enrollment.

Downloadable resources are available in the "Resources" tab of each course module. Click on the file name to download. Note that not all courses offer downloadable materials, and some resources may be viewable only within the platform for copyright reasons.

Yes, UlyBilim is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. We also offer dedicated apps for iOS and Android, which you can download from the App Store or Google Play Store for a better mobile learning experience with offline access capabilities.

Once you've completed all required modules and passed the final assessment (if applicable), your certificate will be automatically generated and available in your "Achievements" section. You can download it as a PDF or share it directly to your LinkedIn profile.
